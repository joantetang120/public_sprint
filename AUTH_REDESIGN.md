## 🎨 **Design System**

### Layout
- **Split screen** (50/50 on desktop)
- **Full width form** on mobile
- **Gradient left side** with branding
- **White/Dark right side** with form

### Colors
- **Gradient**: Primary-600 → Purple-600 → Pink-600
- **Inputs**: Gray-50 (light) / Dark-900 (dark)
- **Borders**: Gray-200 / Dark-800
- **Focus**: Primary-500
- **Errors**: Red-600 / Red-400

### Typography
- **Headings**: Font-black (900 weight)
- **Labels**: Font-semibold
- **Inputs**: Font-medium
- **Links**: Font-semibold

### Spacing
- **Input padding**: py-3, px-12 (with icons)
- **Form spacing**: space-y-5 or space-y-6
- **Section padding**: p-8 or p-12

### Animations
- **Logo**: Rotates 180° on hover
- **Submit button**: Scale on hover
- **Arrow icon**: Translates on hover
- **Form**: Fade in on load
- **Stats/Benefits**: Stagger animation
