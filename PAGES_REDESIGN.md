## 🎨 **Design System**

### Colors
- **Primary**: Blue (#3B82F6)
- **Purple**: (#9333EA)
- **Pink**: (#EC4899)
- **Orange**: (#F97316)
- **Green**: (#10B981)
- **Red**: (#EF4444)

### Typography
- **Headings**: Black weight (900)
- **Body**: Medium/Semibold
- **Small text**: Regular/Medium

### Spacing
- Consistent 8px grid
- Generous padding (p-6, p-8, p-12)
- Large gaps (gap-6, gap-8)

### Borders
- 2px borders on cards
- Rounded corners (rounded-2xl, rounded-3xl)
- Hover effects (border color change)

### Shadows
- Subtle shadows on cards
- Larger shadows on hover (hover:shadow-xl)

### Animations
- Staggered entrance animations
- Hover scale effects
- Smooth transitions
